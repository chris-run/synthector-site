Synthector is a data transformation layer that sits before AI systems—designed to irreversibly transform unstructured text and audio that may contain personal data into privacy‑reduced outputs (and optional synthetic artifacts).

Email: Synthector@gmail.com Read the overview

[1] What it is

  • A transformation layer designed to reduce handling and retention of personal data before downstream AI usage.
  • Built for unstructured inputs (text + audio) and privacy‑reduced outputs.
  • Intended to support safer experimentation and audit‑friendly evidence from early deployments.

[2] What it is not

  • Not a data warehouse.
  • Not a model training platform.
  • Not long‑term storage for customer content.
