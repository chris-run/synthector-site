Synthector

unstructured → privacy-safe → AI-ready

Synthector transforms unstructured text and audio into privacy-safe data so teams can use AI without carrying raw sensitive content through their systems.

Contact Overview

[1] What it is

  • Privacy-first transformation infrastructure for unstructured inputs (text + audio).
  • Designed for auditability and predictable behavior.
  • Built around a “zero retention by default” posture for customer content.

[2] Why it exists

  • AI is powerful, but raw inputs often contain Personally Identifiable Information (PII).
  • Most teams want insights without increasing privacy and compliance risk.
  • Synthector helps reduce risk before downstream data processing or storage.
